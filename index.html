Features

Small utility. Fast workflow.

Clipboard history

Keep recent copied items available after your system clipboard changes.

Search

Find clips by typing words, phrases, URLs, commands, or fragments.

Reusable snippets

Bring back replies, notes, code, links, and other text you use often.

Local-first

Designed around keeping clipboard data on your Mac, without a required cloud account.

Encryption and privacy

Your clipboard should stay yours.

Local storage

Clipever is built as a local-first app. Clipboard history is intended to stay on the Mac where it is captured.

At rest

For device-level encryption, use macOS FileVault and a strong login password to protect local data on disk.

In transit

This website is static and served by GitHub Pages over HTTPS/TLS. Clipever does not require a hosted clipboard service.

Backups

If you back up or sync app data with Apple services, protection follows your Apple ID, iCloud, and device security settings.

No tracking

This site has no cookies, ads, analytics scripts, or tracking pixels.
