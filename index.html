Privacy and encryption

Built around local control.

Clipever is described as a local-first clipboard companion. That means the privacy model is intentionally simple: your clipboard history should be stored on the device you use, and Clipever should not need a hosted account, analytics profile, or remote clipboard database to do its job.

Clipboard contents can include passwords, tokens, private messages, and confidential work. Treat any clipboard manager as sensitive software and avoid saving secrets unless you intentionally choose to.

Privacy policy

Clipever does not sell personal data. Clipever should not collect clipboard contents for advertising, tracking, or profiling. If you contact support by email, your message, email address, diagnostic details, and any information you choose to include may be used only to respond to your request and improve the app.

This website is static and does not include cookies, ad trackers, or analytics scripts. GitHub may process standard server logs when serving GitHub Pages.
